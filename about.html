Our Story

The Search for Purity

Our journey began deep in the treacherous ranges of the Himalayas and Karakoram. We sought to discover the ancient secrets of vitality used by mountain ascetics for thousands of years.

What we found was Shilajit – a rare, potent biomass oozing from high-altitude rocks. Instead of mass-producing, we partnered with local families who have harvested Shilajit sustainably for generations. They hand-pick the raw resin and purify it using nothing but fresh mountain spring water and the warmth of the sun.

  • Cold-filtered Purification
